Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment?

Appointments are recommended at most salons to guarantee a time with your preferred stylist. Call (858) 775-9971 to check current availability and book a time that works for you.

Do you specialize in Asian hair textures?

Yes. Tiffany Hair's cutting and styling approach has an Asian hair specialty, and the salon's K-Beauty inspired techniques are tailored to a range of hair textures and densities.

What should I bring to a color appointment?

Reference photos are always helpful for color and styling appointments so your stylist can understand the look you're going for before you begin.

Is the salon good for kids' haircuts?

Yes, kids' and boys' haircuts are part of the regular service menu, with patient, friendly service for younger clients.

Where is Tiffany Hair located?

The salon is located at 7373 Clairemont Mesa Blvd, Ste 105C, San Diego, in the Kearny Mesa / Clairemont Mesa area, see the Visit section below for directions.
